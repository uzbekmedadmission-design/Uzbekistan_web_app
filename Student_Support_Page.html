Student Support Services

Comprehensive assistance for Indian students at Fergana Medical Institute

Your Well-being is Our Priority

At Fergana Medical Institute, we understand that studying abroad can be challenging. That's why we provide extensive support services to ensure your academic success and personal well-being throughout your MBBS journey.

From the moment you arrive until your graduation, our dedicated support team is available to assist you with any academic, personal, or logistical challenges you may face.

Guidance and mentorship from experienced Indian faculty members throughout your studies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there Indian food available on campus?

Yes, we have dedicated Indian mess facilities serving authentic Indian cuisine. There are also Indian restaurants near the campus.

How do you help students prepare for NEXT exam?

We conduct regular NEXT coaching sessions from the first year, with special focus on exam patterns, syllabus coverage, and mock tests.

What medical facilities are available for students?

The campus has a medical center with doctors available 24/7. For specialized care, we have tie-ups with nearby hospitals.

Can parents visit students in Uzbekistan?

Absolutely! We assist with visitor visas and can arrange accommodation for visiting parents.

Have More Questions About Student Life?

Our student support team is ready to answer all your queries and help you prepare for your MBBS journey in Uzbekistan.
