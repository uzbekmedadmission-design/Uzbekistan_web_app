Admission Process at Fergana Medical Institute

Simple, transparent process with complete guidance from our team

Eligibility Criteria

Must be NEET Qualified

Indian students must have qualified the NEET exam to be eligible for admission at Fergana Medical Institute of Public Health.

Step-by-Step Admission Process

1

Document Submission

Submit the following documents:

  • 10th and 12th Mark Sheets
  • NEET Scorecard
  • Passport Copy
2

Admission Letter

Receive your official admission letter from the university within 7-10 working days after document verification.

3

Passport Application

If you don't have a passport, we'll guide you through the application process to obtain one.

4

Visa Application

Apply for a student visa with full support from the university and our team.

5

Travel & Arrival

UMA will assist with travel arrangements and airport pickup in Uzbekistan.
