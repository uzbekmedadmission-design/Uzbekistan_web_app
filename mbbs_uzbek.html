About Fergana Medical Institute

Fergana Medical Institute of Public Health in Uzbekistan is one of the oldest and most reputed government medical institutes in the country. It is NMC and WHO approved and recognized by several major international medical councils and organizations.

This institute offers a world-class medical education at an affordable cost. The tuition fees are very low compared to other countries, making it a preferred destination for Indian students. The infrastructure is modern, and clinical exposure starts right from the first year, giving students hands-on experience early in their medical journey.
