About Uzbek Med Admission

A Renowned Education Consultancy service provider for medical studies in Uzbekistan

A huge number of medical aspirants across India and South Asia have chosen UMA to pursue their dream of studying in a medical university. At UMA, our team of experts, doctors and teachers ensure high quality of service, greater transparency in admission process and assistance with a human touch.

UMA (UZBEK MED ADMISSION) is pioneered to facilitate MBBS studies for students who think that study MBBS abroad is just a dream. We boost the confidence in aspirants who wish to receive world-class medical education and pave their path towards Top MBBS Universities in Uzbekistan with a very affordable cost.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide one-stop solutions to medical students. We cover end-to-end services for admission in medical institutes for MBBS in Uzbekistan which is becoming the epicenter of MBBS studies in Central Asia.
