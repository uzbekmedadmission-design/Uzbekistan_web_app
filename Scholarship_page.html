Scholarship Opportunities at Fergana Medical Institute

Financial support for deserving and talented students

Merit-Based Scholarships

Fergana Medical Institute of Public Health promotes academic excellence by offering scholarships and fee waivers based on academic performance and other achievements. The university encourages students to perform well both academically and in extracurricular fields through these merit-based rewards.

Academic Excellence

Scholarships for students with outstanding academic records

Sports Achievements

Special scholarships for students with achievements in sports

Cultural Talents

Recognition for students excelling in cultural activities

Available Scholarship Programs

Merit Scholarship

Up to 50% tuition fee waiver for students with exceptional academic performance in 12th grade (Minimum 85% aggregate)

Sports Scholarship

25-50% fee waiver for students who have represented state/national level in any sport

Cultural Scholarship

20-30% fee waiver for students with achievements in cultural activities at state/national level

Need-Based Scholarship

Financial aid for economically disadvantaged students with good academic records

How to Apply for Scholarships

1

Check Eligibility

Review the scholarship criteria to ensure you qualify for the program

2

Prepare Documents

Gather required documents including academic records, achievement certificates, etc.

3

Submit Application

Complete the scholarship application form and submit with supporting documents

4

Interview (if required)

Some scholarships may require an interview with the scholarship committee

5

Receive Decision

Scholarship decisions are typically announced within 4-6 weeks of application

Documents Required for Scholarship Application

12th Grade Mark Sheet (Certified Copy)
Achievement Certificates (Sports/Cultural)
Passport Copy
Scholarship Application Form
Income Certificate (for Need-Based Scholarships)
Medical Fitness Certificate

Tips for a Successful Scholarship Application

  • Apply Early: Many scholarships have limited funds, so early applications have better chances
  • Complete Documentation: Ensure all required documents are included and properly certified
  • Highlight Achievements: Clearly showcase your academic and extracurricular accomplishments
  • Write a Strong Statement: For scholarships requiring essays, make your personal statement compelling
  • Meet Deadlines: Submit your application well before the deadline to avoid last-minute issues

Want to Know More About Scholarships?

Our counselors can guide you through the scholarship application process and help you identify the best opportunities.
